Step 2: Login into the app

If you do not have an account you can signup You will see the home page of the app

Step 3: Create new connection (part 1)

Click the create new connection button in the home home page You will see the empty form

Step 4: Create Google Sheet

In the previous form, you can see the Google account you need to share your sheet access to. Copy the email address

Create Google Sheet

Share Google Sheet access with the service account email

You will need to get the (Google Sheet ID) from the URL You will also need the FormSG Secret Key

Step 5: Create connection (part 2++)

Fill up the rest of the form with the information required You can get the (Google Sheet ID) from the URL You will also need the (FormSG Secret Key) Telegram GroupID will be explained in a different page Once you have created the connection, you can see the following page

Step 6: Test Connection (Google Sheet part)

At this step, click the Test Google Sheet Connection button You will see the following popup once the test passes And your Google Sheet will have a new entry If you have failed to set the share access, you will see the error. If you have the wrong Google Sheet ID, you will see a different error.

Do not proceed without solving the previous problems

Step 7: Settings on the FormSG

Go to the setting tab on your FormSG Form you want to link to the Google Sheet Scroll down to the webhook section Copy the webhook URL from the ('app') Paste the webhook URL into FormSG setting Note, you might need to deactivate your form to do this step Activate your form Once the form is activated, go to your form

Step 8: Test Connection (FormSG Part)

And make a test to ensure the link between FormSG and Google Sheet works
